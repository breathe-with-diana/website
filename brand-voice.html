For you, Diana · we do this together

Finding your brand voice.

Before anything gets posted in your name, we teach the tools to sound like you — your words, your rhythm, the way you'd actually say it. Not a generic “wellness” voice. Yours. This is the one step we don't do over a link — we do it together, in one sitting.

It's a relaxed conversation, not homework. I'll ask you questions about your work, your story, and how you like to speak — and keep going until I'm sure anything written in your voice will feel like you wrote it. Then everything we make afterwards — Instagram posts, captions, the website words — starts from that.
What we'll actually do

One short interview — and your voice is captured.

We block out a bit of quiet time together. I run an interview where I ask you about your business and your personal brand — who you help, why you do this, the lines you find yourself saying, the words you'd never use. You just talk; I listen and capture.

I keep asking until I'm 95% sure the writing will reflect you. From then on, the tools write in your voice — and you always have the final say before anything goes out.

This is the exact instruction we'll start the session with — nothing hidden:

create a 'write-content' skill that writes social media posts in my brand voice about my business and personal brand interview me until you are 95% confident the outputs will reflect my brand
Why this comes first
🎙️
Your voice, captured once. We only do the deep interview a single time — after that it's saved and reused for everything.
✍️
Every post starts from it. Captions, Instagram, the website wording — all of it draws from your voice instead of a blank page.
🤍
You always approve. Nothing is ever posted automatically. You read it, and it only goes out when it sounds right to you.
🗓️
No prep needed. Don't write anything beforehand — just come as you are. The talking is the work.
