Your Instagram bios · English & Spanish

Fourteen ways to introduce yourself.

Your bio is the first thing a new person reads, three short lines under your name. We wrote fourteen very different directions in two batches: first seven steady ones, from plain to warm, then seven bolder ones built to catch the eye and spark a little curiosity. Each is in English and Spanish, all saying the same true things in different tones, so you can hear which one sounds like you.

How to choose: there's no wrong answer here. Tap This one under the directions you'd actually use, you can pick more than one, or say "the English from 2 with the last line from 5." A few things worth knowing about Instagram bios:
  • Each bio fits the 150-character limit, the count is shown under every draft.
  • Your name in bold (Breathe with Diana / Respira con Diana) sits above the bio already, so the bio doesn't need to repeat your name.
  • The one clickable link lives in its own field, not in the text. The little ↓ arrow just points down to it.
  • The white heart 🤍 is your brand's one emoji. Keep it, swap it, or drop it, your call.
