A first look, just for you

Three directions for your new website.

Hola Diana. Below are three different ideas for how your website could feel. They are early drafts, playgrounds to explore, not the finished site. Each one takes the same words, photos and reviews we already have and treats them in a completely different way.

Open each one, move through it slowly, and notice what your body likes. You do not need to choose a whole one. Tell Daniel the moments and details you love from any of them, and the real site will be built from your favorite pieces.

Direction A

The Long Exhale

The whole page breathes with you, slowly, like a resting chest. The little dandelion from your logo becomes something you can rest your thumb on and breathe along with.

Try this: rest and hold your thumb on the small seed just under the top.

Open this one
Direction B

One Point of Ink

Calm and still, like a beautifully printed book. Almost nothing moves, except the full stop at the end of your headline, which quietly breathes.

Try this: tap the period after "Coming home to your body."

Open this one
Direction C

The First Question

The page greets you by the time of day and asks one gentle question before anything else. When you answer, it quietly softens and speaks back to you.

Try this: tap one of the four answers to the first question.

Open this one

A few gentle notes

  • These work best on your phone, the same way most people will find you.
  • Tap and scroll and play. Nothing here is live yet, and you cannot break anything.
  • Each one has a small control near the top to turn the movement off, in case you ever want it perfectly still.
  • There is no right answer. Just notice what feels like you.

When you are ready, send Daniel a voice note or message with what you loved, a whole direction, or just a moment here and there. That is exactly how we will shape the real one.
