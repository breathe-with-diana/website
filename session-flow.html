1 · Your journey, start to finish

Eight little steps. Most of it you already do — this just names it so we can hang the reminders on it:

1
Someone’s curious

A DM, a referral, a message to Iwantto@. You reply warmly and offer a free short call.

2
Free intro call~15 min

What they’re looking for · a gentle check for anything that wouldn’t be safe · you explain the work, including that it can be intense · the price comes up here, not on the website · you book the session.

3
Booked — date & price agreed

You confirm the day, and take their email & phone. That’s the moment the reminders below get set.

4
The day beforeDay −1

A short reminder to them + what to bring; you prepare the space.

5
The sessionyou fill this in

Your 2–3 hour private session — pre-brief and post-support included. This part is yours — I’ve left it open. A rough shape to confirm or rewrite: settle in → pre-brief / intention → the breathwork → rest & integration → grounding → a short talk after. Tell me the real steps.

6
AftercareDay +1

Just a check-in — “how are you feeling today?” The first day or two can still be a lot. No ask, only care.

7
Review request~Day +3

Once they feel grounded, you send the review form link in your own words. (The form from the last page.)

8
Stay in touchDay +8

A light nudge while it’s still fresh — “how are you now?”, and the door’s open for a next session.

2 · The reminders that appear when you book

The moment a session is booked, four little reminders drop into your calendar — counted from the session day. They’re for you (the client never sees them); each one is your nudge to reach out personally:

−1day
Prep + remind them

Send a gentle reminder + what to bring; get the space ready.

+1day
Aftercare check-in

“How are you feeling today?” — care only, no ask.

+3days
Send the review form 🤍

Once they’re grounded. The link is sitting right inside the reminder, ready to paste.

+8days
Stay-in-touch nudge

While it’s fresh — “how are you now?”, and gently offer a next session.

These are starting points — you can shift any of them per person. Someone who needs longer to settle? Move the review ask out. The +8 is on the early side on purpose, so the experience is still warm in their memory.

How it works — now vs. later:
For now: when you book someone, you copy four ready-made reminder events onto your calendar (I’ll give you the template — takes a few seconds).
Later: we make it fully automatic — you book the session, and the four reminders create themselves. (That waits until your Google email & calendar are set up; it’s on the to-do list.)
3 · What’s automatic — and what’s always you

The point of all this is to take the remembering and the busywork off your plate — never the human part.

The system does

Sets the reminders · collects the reviews · turns a review into a finished Instagram tile — no copying, no design.

Always you

The actual messages you send · the session itself · and choosing which reviews feel right to share.
